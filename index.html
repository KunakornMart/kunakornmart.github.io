About Me

Engineer by training,
AI builder by practice.

I'm an Electrical & Automation Engineer at Singha Corporation (Boon Rawd Brewery Group), where I've spent four-plus years working across SCADA, Siemens PLC S7-1500, and IoT systems on real production lines — while building the data pipelines and AI tools that make those environments easier to understand and act on.

I recently finished my M.S. in Data Analytics & Data Science from NIDA Thailand. My research went into agentic AI for financial decision-making, Thai NLP benchmarking, streaming analytics, and time-series forecasting. Two papers are currently under journal review.

The through-line across all of it: industrial and operational data is almost always underused. Connect it properly with modern analytics and AI, and there's a lot of room to make systems more useful and decisions more informed.

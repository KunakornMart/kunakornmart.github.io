About

From industrial automation to data and AI systems.

My background started in instrumentation and control engineering, then grew into manufacturing automation, real-time monitoring, data pipelines, and eventually machine learning, NLP, and agentic AI. I am most interested in work that connects software, data, and physical systems in a practical way.

At Singha Corporation, I work in electrical and automation engineering across production operations. That includes SCADA, Siemens PLC environments, IoT-style monitoring flows, and the systems around them that make plant data easier to read, track, and act on.

Alongside that, I completed a master’s degree in Data Analytics and Data Science at NIDA, where my project work focused on agentic AI, Thai NLP, streaming analytics, and time-series forecasting.

The thread across all of my work is fairly consistent: build systems that are technically solid, operationally useful, and clear enough that people can trust what they are seeing.

SCADA, Siemens PLC, IoT monitoring, dashboarding, alerts, production visibility, and plant-floor automation support.

About Me

Engineer by trade,
AI builder by choice.

I'm an Electrical & Automation Engineer at Singha Corporation (Boon Rawd Brewery Group), where I've spent more than four years maintaining, improving, and building around SCADA, Siemens PLC S7-1500, and IoT systems on live manufacturing production lines — while building the data pipelines and AI tools on top of them.

I completed my M.S. in Data Analytics and Data Science at the National Institute of Development Administration (NIDA) in 2025. Research: agentic AI for portfolio management, Thai NLP benchmarking, streaming analytics, and time-series forecasting. Two papers currently under journal review.

Operational data from industrial environments is almost always underused. The gap between what's being measured and what's being acted on is where I work.
